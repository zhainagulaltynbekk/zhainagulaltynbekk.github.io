About Covid-19

The main Symptoms

• High body temperature,loss of taste.

• Cough (dry or with a small amount of phlegm).

• Dyspnea,muscle pain,fatigue.

• Impaired or loss of smell.

Rare Symptoms

• Headache,diarrhea,hemoptysis.

• Chest congestion,nausea,vomiting.

Symptoms may appear within 14 days of contact with an infectious patient. Symptoms are very similar to many respiratory diseases, often mimicking the common cold, and can be similar to the flu.

How is it Transmitted?

• By airborne droplets (when coughing or sneezing).

• By contact (handrails in vehicles, door handles and other contaminated surfaces and objects).

Like other respiratory viruses, coronavirus spreads through droplets that form when an infected person coughs or sneezes.

It can also spread when an infected person touches any contaminated surface, such as a doorknob. People become infected when they touch their mouth, nose, or eyes with contaminated hands.

Steps to Prevent

• When visiting public places (shops, transport, etc.), be sure to use medical disposable or hygienic reusable masks and maintain a social distance of 1.5 - 2 meters.

• Do not touch your eyes, face and mouth with dirty hands.

• Avoid close contact and stay in the same room with people who have visible signs of SARS (coughing, sneezing, nasal discharge).

Others Steps to Prevent

• Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water after returning from the street and contact with people, disinfect gadgets and work surfaces.

• Disinfect gadgets, office equipment and surfaces you touch.

• Stay out of crowded spaces and avoid mass gatherings

• Use only personal hygiene items (towel, toothbrush).

• Limit close hugs and handshakes when greeting when possible.
